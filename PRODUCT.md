# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

People in Dar es Salaam who need an iPhone, iPad, Android phone, Apple Watch, or MacBook repaired. Most visitors arrive on a phone, often with a damaged device, and need to identify the likely repair, understand what happens next, and request an appointment quickly.

## Product Purpose

Present iPhone Service Tanzania as a precise, trustworthy Apple and Android device repair specialist and turn service discovery into a clear booking request. Success means customers can find a relevant repair, understand the inspection process and warranty expectations, and contact or book the team without uncertainty.

## Brand Personality

Precise, reassuring, and polished. The experience should feel premium and technically capable while remaining approachable and locally grounded.

## Anti-references

Do not copy Apple or the Instagram feed directly. Avoid cluttered marketplace layouts, cheap phone-shop aesthetics, oversized mobile cards, repetitive generic feature grids, and form controls that expose long unfiltered native menus.

## Design Principles

1. Put the next useful action within easy reach on a phone.
2. Use progressive disclosure so detailed repair information never blocks discovery.
3. Build trust through clear diagnostics, expectations, and warranty language.
4. Let real repair imagery and concise technical details demonstrate expertise.
5. Keep every interaction fast, touch-friendly, and easy to recover from.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Maintain keyboard support, visible focus states, 44px minimum touch targets, readable contrast, semantic labels, reduced-motion and reduced-transparency fallbacks, and layouts that remain usable from 320px mobile widths upward.
